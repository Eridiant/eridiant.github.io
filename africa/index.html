Visit Kruger National Park

Hear the call of the wild as you explore one of the largest game reserves in Africa. Once inside the grounds, keep your eyes peeled for the Big Five: leopards, lions, elephants, rhinos and buffalos. Some are much harder to spot than others. So, binoculars at the ready!

  • Learn more

Visit Victoria Falls

Welcome to the majestic Victoria Falls! Did you know that the spray from this huge waterfall can be seen from up to 20 or 30 kilometres away – hence the local name Mosi-oa-Tunya (the ‘smoke that thunders’)? Hint: now’s the perfect chance to use that waterproof case you bought for your camera.

  • Learn more

Explore Cape Town

Hey there, cosmopolitan Cape Town! Nestled between flat-topped Table Mountain and the Atlantic, Cape Town is one of the world's most picturesque cities. With outdoor activities a’plenty, you can take your pick from hiking, biking or water sports and make sunny memories that will last a lifetime. On the streets of Cape Town, you’ll find world-class museums, shopping and foodie options too. Sigh, this place is paradise.

Unique experiences

From coming face to face with endangered mountain gorillas in Uganda to tracking elusive rhinos in Zimbabwe, our included and optional activities will give your Africa trip allll of the once-in-a-lifetime feels.

Passion & expertise

In Africa, we hand over to leading safari operator Acacia Africa, whose team of local crew have been delivering epic adventures across the continent for 20 years.

Freedom to explore

No one wants to feel like a sheep. That’s why our Africa trips offer loads of free time to uncover hidden gems, channel your inner explorer and create your very own story, the way you want it to be.

Lasting friendships

Memories fade but Facebook friends are forever. Hit up Africa with other young explorers aged 18-39 and create bonds that last longer than your festival wristband.

Delicious food

You know how it is – ain’t nobody does hearty, rustic eats like Africa. From delicious barbecued meats to traditional pap, Africa packs tasty treats for days. Rest assured we’ll ensure your tummy is just as full as your passport.
